About the Song

Spiral of Ants is the final track in Lemon Demon's 2017 album Spirit Phone, created by Neil Cicierega. The story sounds deep, but really, Neil just wanted to write a song about bugs.

The song describes an ant (you), who doesn't know its purpose in life. Then suddenly, you discover a spiral of ants and feel a new sense of determination: you're convinced that following your peers is your destiny, forget everything else. You laugh at passerby bugs, even though you're just gonna starve and die. Ants actually do this in real life because they are weird and dumb.(2)

In his Spirit Phone commentary, Neil revealed his ultimate songwriting method that he used for this song: I knew this would be a potent metaphor and all I had to do was carefully write around it without giving up that I don’t know what I’m doing.(3)

About Lemon Demon

Lemon Demon is a one-man band, created by Neil Cicierega back in 2003. He has released 7 studio albums under the Lemon Demon name. Spirit Phone, his latest album, was released in 2016, after a whopping 8 years of wait since his previous album, View-Monster. He is currently working on a new album, currently untitled, and he needs to post it now so I can be happy.
